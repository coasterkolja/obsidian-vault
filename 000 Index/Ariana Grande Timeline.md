# Awiana Gwande Timeline

![[arian grandel.jpg]]

- **1993** – Born on June 26 in Boca Raton, Florida.
- **2008** – Makes her Broadway debut in the musical _13_.
- **2010** – Cast as Cat Valentine in Nickelodeon’s _Victorious_, gaining popularity with a young audience.
- **2013** – Releases debut album _Yours Truly_, which debuts at No. 1 on the Billboard 200.
- **2014** – Releases second album _My Everything_ with hits like “Problem” and “Bang Bang.”
- **2016** – Drops _Dangerous Woman_, solidifying her as a global pop star.
- **2017** – Manchester Arena bombing occurs during her _Dangerous Woman Tour_; she organizes the _One Love Manchester_ benefit concert.
- **2018** – Releases _Sweetener_, later winning her first Grammy.
- **2019** – Releases _Thank U, Next_, breaking streaming records and achieving huge commercial success.
- **2020** – Collaborates with Lady Gaga on “Rain on Me,” which wins a Grammy Award.
- **2021** – Becomes a coach on _The Voice_ (US) and is added as a playable character/skin in _Fortnite_ during the Rift Tour.
- **2024** – Stars as Glinda in the film adaptation of the musical _Wicked_ (Part One).
