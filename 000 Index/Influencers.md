1. What is mimetic desire?
	- to adopt another persons desire for our own, often without noticing
2. What is “an external mediator of desire”?
	- models outside of our world
	- no possibility of contact with them
	- outside of competition
	- can be real or fictional
3. What is “an internal mediator of desire”?
	- models inside our world
	- rivalry/conflicts
4. What is said about the dangers of external mediators of desire?
	- theres no end of comparing ourselves to them
5. What desires are modelled on social media?
	- lifestyles
	- vacations
	- fashions
	- ways of engagement
	- political preferences
